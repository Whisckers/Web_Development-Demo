Instructions on voting

Voters' Roll

To be able to vote, you must register as a voter. Once you've registered your details appear on the voters'Roll which contains over 20 million names.
The voters' roll helps us plan an election and identify fraud as it tells us how many voters to expect in each district.

Voters' Rights

  • Vote and have your vote counted accurately
  • Vote on a system that is in working condition and that will allow votes to be accurately casted.
  • Vote freely from duress or coercion or intimidation by elections officers or any other person
  • Vote independently and in a safe secure space.
  • Vote and have your vote counted accurately
  • Vote on a system that will keep your identity secure.

Voters' Responsibilities

  • Each voter must bring valid identity documents when casting their vote
  • Know your voting station and stick to it
  • Respect and follow the instructions of the voting online system
  • Report any suspicious activity, problems or violations of election laws to the Electroal Commission
  • Make sure that your voter's form is fully complete before submitting.
  • Make sure you only vote for one constituency; so vote the one you want
  • Make sure all information provided on the voter form is valid and up to date.

When to update your registration details

You must update your registration details when:

  • Your address changes
  • Your ID number changes (e.g. new ID number due to to its expiration.)
  • Your surname changes (e.g. after getting married).
  • When you get acquire a new registration card.

Voting Eligibility

Eligible citizens are required to produce valid National Identity Cards in order to register in accordance with the electoral law. Eligible citizens register at their
place of residence. During registation process, an online form is available where voters will cast their vote independently and privately.

Who can vote?

  • Residents and non-residents CITIZENS of Botswana
  • Botswana CITIZENS who of minimal voting age; 18 years or older
  • Botswana CITIZENS who who are in possession of a National Identity Card valid (omang).
  • Botswana CITIZENS with a valid voter registration card.
  • Passport or Driver’s licences are not allowed.
